### HOW THE BOT WORKS
This bot is entirely driven by Twitch chat! Whenever a mod enters the `!editcom !teams` command in a CQ streamer's chat, the post will tweet a live game update.

### HOW TO CONTRIBUTE
1. Be a mod for your favourite CQ streamer! If you aren't a mod but still want to contribute, please DM `@ChampQueueWard` with your Twitch username.
2. When a new game starts, use https://champions-queue-cmd-builder.vercel.app/ to fill out teams and generate the Twitch command (see link in bio)
3. Enter the command into chat
4. Check `@ChampQueueWard` to see your tweet!

**Note**: please avoid changing the format of the generated command. The bot will likely invalidate the command and won't tweet anything.

### WHY IS THE BOT COMMUNITY DRIVEN
Since access to live CQ game data isn't publicly available, a community-driven approach appears to be the next best alternative.
